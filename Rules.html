FEDERAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION

● Fidelity ● Bravery ● Integrity ●

Celestial City Roleplay

Section 1: General Rules and Conduct

1. Professionalism: Maintain a high level of professionalism in all interactions, whether with colleagues, civilians, or other factions. Conduct yourself with dignity, respect, and integrity at all times.

2. Confidentiality: Safeguard sensitive information obtained during the course of duty and refrain from disclosing it to unauthorized individuals or factions. Respect the privacy rights of individuals and maintain the confidentiality of ongoing investigations.

3. Integrity: Uphold the highest standards of honesty, integrity, and ethical behavior. Avoid conflicts of interest, and never engage in activities that may compromise the integrity of the FBI or its investigations.

4. Compliance: Adhere to all federal, state, and local laws, as well as the policies, procedures, and directives of the FBI Department of Celestial City. Familiarize yourself with relevant laws and regulations to ensure compliance in all aspects of your work.

5. Teamwork: Foster a culture of collaboration, cooperation, and mutual respect within the department. Support your fellow agents, share information and resources, and work together to achieve common goals and objectives.

Section 2: Operational Guidelines

1. Authority: Respect the chain of command and follow the directives of your superiors. Adhere to established protocols and procedures, and seek clarification or guidance when necessary.

2. Use of Force: Exercise discretion and restraint when using force, and ensure that it is always proportionate to the threat faced. Prioritize de-escalation techniques and seek to resolve conflicts peacefully whenever possible.

3. Arrest Procedures: Follow proper arrest procedures in accordance with federal, state, and local laws. Identify yourself as an FBI agent, inform suspects of their rights, and ensure that arrests are conducted in a safe and lawful manner.

4. Evidence Handling: Properly document, collect, preserve, and analyze evidence in accordance with FBI protocols and best practices. Maintain chain of custody at all times to ensure the integrity and admissibility of evidence in court.

4. Surveillance: Conduct surveillance activities in a lawful, ethical, and respectful manner. Obtain necessary warrants and authorizations as required by law, and exercise caution to minimize the risk of infringing upon the privacy rights of individuals.

Section 3: Personal Conduct

1. Appearance: Present yourself in a professional and well-groomed manner while on duty, including wearing the official FBI uniform and badge as required. Maintain a clean and tidy appearance that reflects positively on the FBI and its mission.

2. Off-Duty Conduct: Remember that you are a representative of the FBI at all times, whether on or off duty. Conduct yourself in a manner that upholds the values and principles of the FBI, and avoid engaging in activities that may discredit or undermine the reputation of the department.

3. Allegiance: Demonstrate unwavering loyalty and allegiance to the FBI Department of Celestial City, its mission, and its objectives. Put the interests of the department and the community above personal or factional interests, and avoid engaging in behavior that may create conflicts of interest or compromise your impartiality.

4. Personal Responsibility: Take ownership of your actions and decisions, and accept accountability for any mistakes or errors that may occur. Learn from your experiences, seek feedback and guidance from your peers and superiors, and strive to continuously improve and develop as a professional.

Section 4: Disciplinary Procedures

1. Investigation: Allegations of misconduct or violations of department policies will be thoroughly investigated by the appropriate authorities. Cooperate fully with any investigations and provide truthful and accurate information as required.

2. Penalties: If misconduct is substantiated, disciplinary action may be taken in accordance with departmental policies and procedures. Possible sanctions may include verbal or written reprimands, suspension, demotion, or termination of employment, depending on the severity of the offense.

Appeals: Members have the right to appeal disciplinary decisions through established channels within the FBI Department of Celestial City. Appeals will be reviewed impartially and fairly, and all relevant evidence and arguments will be considered in reaching a final decision.
